Frequently Asked Questions
About Our Chatbot

How big is the chatbot?

The chatbot is designed to be lightweight and easily adaptable to your needs.

How long does it take to train the chatbot?

As the chatbot does not utilize a neural network, there is no training time.

Where can I find datasets for use as an input text file?

Datasets can be found anywhere on the internet on websites like kaggle.com. You are also able to create your own based off of your own data.

How can I deploy the chatbot on my website or app?

The chatbot is designed to be able to be easily integrated into any app and/or website. You may also contact us for more info regarding any special agreements.

How do I use your chatbot?

There is no programming experience required to create your very own chatbot. You only need a valid text file as an input, and your very own chatbot will be available for chatting through our interface.
